Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a consultation? +

Fill out our contact form, call our hotline, or send a WhatsApp message. We'll respond within 24 hours to schedule your appointment.

Are your surgeons board-certified? +

Yes, all surgeons and specialists are board-certified and have undergone rigorous training both locally and internationally.

How long is recovery after cosmetic surgery? +

Recovery varies by procedure. Minor procedures may require 3–7 days, while major surgeries can take 2–6 weeks. We provide personalized post-op care plans.

Do you offer financing options? +

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for select procedures. Our team will walk you through all available options during your consultation.

What should I expect during my first visit? +

A comprehensive consultation where we assess your goals, review your medical history, and recommend the most suitable treatment plan. No obligation to proceed.

Is HBOT safe for everyone? +

HBOT is safe for most people but certain conditions may require medical clearance. We conduct a thorough evaluation before any HBOT session.
